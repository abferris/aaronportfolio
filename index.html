Portfolio

Hi, My name is Aaron Ferris. I am a junior web developer with a background in engineering, leadership and customer service. I grew up in Seattle near the University of Washington. In first grade, my best friend said he wanted to be an engineer, and I decided to explore the world of knowledge as well. Ever since then, I've loved mental challenges and solving them, however I have always been primarily a social butterfly.

For my higher education, I attended University of Portland's Donald P. Shiley School of Engineering, and completed a BSME. While working on my final project, I felt isolated from people and instead of pursuing a career with my degree I decided to move to something more people oriented, which eventually landed me a job in retail while working in the community coaching Ultimate Frisbee.

While I did well and progressed to a manager, but I never felt completely challenged enough. In the natural progression of that career, I only saw myself becoming more miserable as a store manager solely concerned with the store’s sales, and decided to move to something else.

My brother had been bothering me to try coding for at least a year and a half, and after talking with people, I decided it was right for me. The challenge and the methodical approach was appealing to me, but I was also pleasantly surprised by how closely developers work together in teams

.

My excellence at isolating and understanding a problem from engineering and my experience in retail, understanding the customer’s needs, building and working with a team has placed me in an excellent place not only to be excited and challenged, but to also thrive in an ever changing environment.
